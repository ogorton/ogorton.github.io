Summary

My dissertation work tackles an open question in the theoretical description of beta-delayed neutron emission by integrating nuclear structure and reactions, by connecting the nuclear shell-model with the Hauser-Feshbach description of nuclear reactions.

My research career has developed on a number of topics in related fields such as the search for dark matter, nuclear data and evaluations, uncertainty quantification, and machine learning. All of my work involves scientific and high performance computing.
